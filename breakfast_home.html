Mote Hin Gar

  • 450 g Fish (sea bass or tilapia or any choice of fish)

  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt
  • Turmeric Powder
  • 1/3 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 cup Oil
  • 5 hard boiled eggs or more/less
  • ~10 Shallots
